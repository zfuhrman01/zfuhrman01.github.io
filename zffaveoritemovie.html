Clueless

My favorite Movie is Clueless. It was written and directed by Amy Heckerling. Its cast features Alicia Silverstone, Paul Rudd, and Stacy Dash.

Clueless is a movie about a highschooler named Cher, who experiences love for the first time. It also covers her struggles with friendship and family as she learns how to navigate highschool
